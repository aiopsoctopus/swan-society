The problem

Book clubs don't die from bad books. They die from logistics.

📚

Picking the book

Someone has to source candidates, poll the group, and tally votes — every single month.

💬

The group chat spiral

RSVPs, who's hosting, who's bringing snacks — all buried in a thread nobody can find later.

❓

Showing up unprepared

No discussion questions, no plan — just an awkward silence and a glass of wine.

🔥

The burnout

One person carries it all, every month, until they quietly stop — and the club dissolves.

How it works

One loop. Beginning to end.

1

AI picks the shortlist

Based on your club's favorite genres, we suggest 3 new releases worth voting on.

2

Everyone votes

Members vote on the shortlist. The winner is selected automatically — no spreadsheet required.

3

RSVPs, sorted

Date, time, location — members respond in one tap. No more "wait, are we still doing this Thursday?"

4

Show up prepared

AI-generated discussion questions, ready the moment the book is chosen. No prep work, no blank stares.
